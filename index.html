Fernanda Da Silva

Registered Occupational Therapist

Hi there! I'm Fernanda, and I am a Registered Occupational Therapist with a passion for pediatrics. My mission is to help kids participate in meaningful activities while valuing integrity, family-centered care, and compassion. If you are looking for support as a parent or need ongoing therapy for your child, you're in the right place!

I provide occupational therapy assessment and intervention for babies, kids, and teens in Ontario, practicing in The Greater Toronto area. I also offer parent consultation sessions to share strategies on supporting your child with a variety of needs.

Areas of Expertise

Handwriting

Supporting children to develop proper handwriting skills, letter formation, pencil grip, and writing endurance for academic success.

Fine Motor Skills

Enhancing hand strength, finger coordination, and dexterity through play-based activities to support self-care and academic tasks.

Play Skills

Developing social play, imaginative play, turn-taking, and peer interaction skills that build friendships and communication.

Emotional Regulation

Teaching children coping strategies, self-calming techniques, and emotional awareness to manage big feelings and behaviors.

Toileting

Supporting families through toilet training, addressing sensory challenges, and developing independence in bathroom routines.

Sleep

Helping children and families establish consistent bedtime routines, address sleep challenges, and improve sleep quality for better daily functioning.

Anxiety

Providing evidence-based strategies, relaxation techniques, and environmental modifications to help children manage anxiety and build confidence.

School Support

Providing classroom accommodations, organizational strategies, and collaboration with teachers to support academic and social success.

Sensory Processing

Addressing sensory sensitivities and seeking behaviors, creating sensory diets, and developing personalized coping strategies for daily life.

Feeding & Picky Eating

Supporting feeding development using the SOS Approach, addressing oral motor skills and food aversions.

Specialized support for picky eaters and developing healthy eating habits.

Autism

Supporting children with autism spectrum disorder and their families.

Certified in the PLAY Project - a relationship-based early intervention for children with autism.

ADHD

Supporting children with ADHD and their families through evidence-based strategies.

Helping improve daily functioning and academic success through targeted interventions.
