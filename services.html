Services & Fees

I provide comprehensive occupational therapy services for infants, children, and adolescents throughout the Greater Toronto Area. My services are designed to support your child's development and help them achieve their full potential in all areas of life.

Comprehensive Assessments

Thorough evaluation to understand your child's strengths, challenges, and developmental needs.

  • Developmental Assessment
  • Sensory Processing Assessment
  • Fine Motor Skills Assessment
  • Feeding Assessment (SOS Approach)
  • Handwriting Assessment
  • School Readiness Assessment
  • Play Skills Assessment

Individual Therapy Sessions

One-on-one therapy sessions tailored to your child's specific needs and goals.

  • Sensory Integration Therapy
  • Fine Motor Development
  • Handwriting Intervention
  • Feeding Therapy
  • Social Skills Development
  • Emotional Regulation Training
  • Play-Based Intervention

PLAY Project Services

Specialized early intervention for children with autism using relationship-based approaches.

  • PLAY Project Assessment
  • Individual PLAY Project Sessions
  • Parent Coaching & Training
  • Home Program Development
  • Progress Monitoring
  • Team Consultation

Parent & Family Services

Education and support for parents and families to implement strategies at home.

  • Parent Consultation Sessions
  • Home Program Development
  • Strategy Implementation Training
  • Family Education Sessions
  • Caregiver Support Groups
  • Sibling Support

School & Community Support

Collaboration with educators and other professionals to support your child across environments.

  • School Consultation
  • Teacher Training
  • Classroom Accommodations
  • IEP/504 Plan Support
  • Transition Planning
  • Community Integration

Specialized Programs

Targeted interventions for specific conditions and challenges.

  • Autism Spectrum Support
  • ADHD Management
  • Anxiety & Emotional Regulation
  • Sleep Difficulties
  • Toileting Support
  • Developmental Delays
